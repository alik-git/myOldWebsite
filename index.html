Ali Kuwajerwala

I just completed my position as researcher at the Robotics Vision & Learning Lab at the University of Toronto, and I am actively seeking software engineering or research roles at this time. You can find my resume here.

If you have any such opportunities available please email me at ali.kuwajerwala@mail.utoronto.ca

Previously I worked as a Software Developer at Epson Canada and a Teaching Assistant at the University of Toronto Computer Science Department, where I also completed my Bachelors Degree, majoring in Computer Science and Math.

Upon graduating I received the NSERC Undergraduate Student Research Award which funded my current research where I was supervised by Prof. Florian Shkurti.

My research/career interests lie within machine learning and robotics, specifically I have worked on several projects ranging from safety analysis for autonomous systems; autonomous driving; reinforcement learning; to neural network interpretability. I also make android apps, and you can view all my projects on my github profile.
